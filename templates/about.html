About Us

Welcome to our Netflix Dashboard Project – a data-driven web application designed to analyze and visualize Netflix content trends. Our goal is to provide users with an interactive platform where they can explore Netflix’s library through charts, graphs, and insights.This project is built using Python, Django, and Power BI (or other visualization tools) to showcase how data science and web development come together. Through this dashboard, users can: Track movie vs. TV show distribution Explore content by country, genre, and release year Identify top-rated titles and popular categories Gain insights into Netflix’s global expansion This is not an official Netflix product – it’s an academic project developed for learning and demonstration purposes. Our vision is to help learners and analysts better understand how to integrate data visualization with full-stack web development
